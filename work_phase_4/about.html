About Us

We bring together international students across Brisbane to create a supportive network where everyone belongs.

Our Mission

Global Community Connect empowers international students to build meaningful connections, access resources, and thrive in Brisbane. We believe studying abroad is about more than academics‚Äîit's about community, belonging, and growth.

What We Offer

Click on each section to learn more about how we support international students.

Build meaningful relationships with fellow international students, alumni, and mentors who understand the unique challenges and opportunities of studying abroad. Our Connect platform is the heart of our community.

Features include:

  • Discussion Forums: Ask questions, share advice, and participate in conversations about academic life, career planning, cultural experiences, and daily life in Brisbane.
  • Member Profiles: Discover students and alumni from your university, program, or home country. See their stories, interests, and experiences.
  • Peer Support: Connect with others who've faced similar challenges, from finding accommodation to navigating visa requirements.
  • Networking Opportunities: Build professional and personal relationships that extend beyond your studies.

Participate in a vibrant calendar of events designed to help you connect, learn, and celebrate your culture while discovering new ones. From casual meetups to professional workshops, there's always something happening.

Event types include:

  • Cultural Celebrations: Share your heritage and learn about other cultures through food, music, and traditions. Events like Lunar New Year, Diwali, and cultural exchange dinners bring our diverse community together.
  • Career Workshops: Develop professional skills through resume writing sessions, interview preparation, networking events, and industry talks from professionals who understand the international student experience.
  • Social Gatherings: Make friends at casual meetups, game nights, outdoor activities, and study groups. Perfect for settling into life in Brisbane.
  • Welcome Events: New to Brisbane? Our orientation sessions and welcome mixers help you meet others and learn about student life in Australia.
  • Submit Your Own: Have an idea? Use our event submission form to organize your own community events.

Navigate life in Brisbane with confidence using our comprehensive guides and resources. We've compiled practical information to help you with everyday challenges, written in clear, accessible language.

Resource categories:

  • Housing & Accommodation: Find your perfect place to live with guides on rental agreements, tenant rights, safe neighborhoods, and tips from other students. Includes a step-by-step checklist to make the search less overwhelming.
  • Healthcare & Wellbeing: Understand the Australian healthcare system, find local doctors, learn how to use your insurance, and access mental health support. Emergency information is prominently displayed.
  • Budgeting & Finance: Manage your money effectively with budgeting templates, banking basics, student discount guides, and tips for saving on everyday expenses. Visual infographics make financial planning easier.
  • Transportation: Get around Brisbane confidently with public transport guides, student concession information, and tips for navigating the city.
  • Academic Support: Links to university resources, study tips, and information about academic integrity and support services.

Advance your career and personal development through opportunities specifically curated for international students. We understand visa restrictions and help you find pathways that work for your situation.

Opportunity types:

  • Internships & Part-Time Jobs: Browse listings that clearly indicate visa requirements, work hour restrictions, and whether positions are suitable for international students. Opportunities span various industries and skill levels.
  • Mentorship Program: Connect with alumni and professionals who've walked the same path. Our mentors provide career guidance, industry insights, and support for navigating the Australian job market. Each mentor profile shows their background, expertise, and how they can help.
  • Volunteering: Give back to the community while building your resume and expanding your network. Volunteer opportunities help you integrate into Australian society, develop new skills, and make meaningful connections with locals.
  • Professional Development: Access workshops, webinars, and skill-building sessions that enhance your employability and prepare you for your career goals.
  • Alumni Success Stories: Learn from graduates who've successfully transitioned from student to professional life in Australia or abroad.

Our Partnership

Global Community Connect is developed in collaboration with the Connect Communities Initiative (CCI), a non-profit dedicated to fostering social cohesion across Australia. This platform is completely free and non-commercial, built with accessibility and inclusivity at its core.
