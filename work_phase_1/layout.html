Layout Example

This page demonstrates the layout structure of the website.

  • Mobile Layout:
    • Full height: 100vh
    • Content width: 80vw
    • Side margins: 10vw (left and right)
    • Button width: spans across the content area
  • Desktop Layout:
    • Content width: 60vw
    • Side margins: 20vw (left and right)
    • Button width: 20% of the content area
