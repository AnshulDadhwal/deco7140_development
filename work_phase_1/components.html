Components

This page outlines the components used in the style guide.

Buttons

  • Padding:
    • Top/Bottom: 10px
    • Left/Right: 20px
  • Styles:
    • Filled (solid background)
    • Outlined (border only)
    • Full-width button
    • Hover state: raised with shadow

Card – Mobile

  • Title spacing: 1.6rem from top
  • Horizontal padding: 1rem (left & right)
  • Button spacing: 1rem below text

Card – Desktop

  • Title spacing: 1.6rem from top
  • Left padding: 3rem
  • Right padding: 2rem
  • Button positioned under text with consistent spacing
