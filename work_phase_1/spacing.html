Spacing

This page outlines the spacing guidelines used in the style guide.

  • Standard space between elements: 16px = 1rem
  • Headings 1, 2, and 3:
    • Space above: 1.5rem
    • Space below: 0.75rem
  • Paragraph spacing:
    • Additional spacing at the bottom of each paragraph: 1.6rem
  • Inside containers:
    • Left padding: 1rem
    • Right padding: 1rem
    • Top padding: 1rem
    • Bottom padding: 1rem
  • Between sections (<section>):
    • Space between sections: 2rem
